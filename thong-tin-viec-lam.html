Địa điểm làm việc

- Hà Nội: TNR Building - 54A Nguyen Chi Thanh - Dong Da - Hanoi, Đống Đa

Mô tả công việc

Defining and building marketing initiatives:
• Develop go-to-market plans including market and competitive analysis, user research, product positioning, and communications strategy
 

Analyzing product and marketing actions through complete data analysis:
• Follow up and analyze product metrics to develop understanding about company products  and compare them with market trends.
• Monitor engagement and daily game activity to evaluate the impact of new content and marketing actions 
 

Collaborate with Product Team to align production efforts with profitability: 
• Act as the eyes and ears of the product team by understanding deep local cultures and user behaviors to build strategies and growth plans in target markets. 
• Participate in game development by making recommendations to improve user experience, engagement, retention, share-ability, and to better integrate in-game promotional events and marketing mechanisms.

Yêu cầu ứng viên

• Critical thinking, self-motivated, result-oriented, and leadership potential 
• Strong communication skills; ability to influence, convince and be assertive
• Fresh Graduates or candidates with up to 2-year working experiences (Bachelor or Master Degree from all Universities) 
• Fluent in English or equivalent to IELTS 7.0 
• Be able to work full-time from July 2022

Preferable: 
• Basic Knowledge or Practical experiences in Digital Marketing, Data Analysis, Market Research…
• Passionate about online gaming

Quyền lợi

Learning & Development Opportunities:

• Equipped with industry knowledge and technical job skills through intensive training classes & practical exercises.
• Explore your own potentials and identify your strengths through each training period.
• Develop teamwork, time management, project coordination skills 
• Working in an intercultural environment with the opportunity to work with foreign colleagues and partners. 
• Have opportunities to become Market Leaders who  develop the point of view as to where the market is and where it should go
• Act as a consultant for the Country Manager, Product Manager to have successful plans and campaigns.


Company Mission & Culture: 
• Become a member of Vietnam's first Unicorn Startup - Top 02 of most favorite technology companies in Vietnam.
• Dynamic working environment with the "Go Global" Mission. 
• Friendly, supportive, and intercultural working environment.

Benefits: 
• Attractive offer package including equipped Macbook Pro and approximate 12.000.000vnđ monthly salary during the training period. 
• Performance appraisal with well-deserved income and benefits (Teambuilding, Elite fitness gym card, international conferences,...) for official employees 
• Chance to travel and explore different cultures around the world

Giới thiệu

VNG là một trong những doanh nghiệp đầu tiên của Việt Nam đi tiên phong trong ngành công nghiệp Internet. Trẻ trung, năng động, nhiệt huyết, không ngừng phát triển và học tập những công nghệ mới, chúng tôi đã đạt nhiều thành tựu về công nghệ số cho các nhu cầu khác nhau với 3 loại hình:

- Nội dung số (Zing.vn..)

- Giải trí trực tuyến, liên kết cộng đồng (Zalo, Zingme)

- Thương mại điện tử (123pay, 123phim) 

cùng các sản phẩm Game nổi bật: Kiếm Thế, Võ Lâm Truyền Kỳ....
